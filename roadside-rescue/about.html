About Roadside Rescue

We are Nashik's premier tech-enabled roadside assistance provider, dedicated to turning your breakdown into a minor inconvenience, not a major crisis.

Our Mission: Your Peace of Mind

At Roadside Rescue, our mission is simple: to provide fast, reliable, and professional vehicle breakdown assistance to drivers throughout Nashik. We understand that being stranded is stressful. That’s why we leverage technology and a network of expert mechanics to get you back on the road safely and swiftly. We’re not just a service; we’re your trusted partner on the road.

Stranded? Don't Wait.

Our team is on standby 24/7, ready to get you moving again.
